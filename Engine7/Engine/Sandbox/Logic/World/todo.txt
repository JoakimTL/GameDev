
Tiles should have a terrain type.
 - Plains
 - Forest
 - Mountain
 - Water
 - Desert
 - Swamp
 ...

Tiles should have names, these should be randomly generated based on the terrain type.
 - Plains: Green Hills, Rolling Hills, Flatlands
 - Forest: Dark Woods, Enchanted Forest, Ancient Grove
 - Mountain: Snowy Peaks, Rocky Mountains, Craggy Hills
 - Water: Crystal Lake, Serene River, Deep Ocean
 - Desert: Sandy Dunes, Rocky Wastes, Dry Plains
 - Swamp: Murky Marshes, Boggy Lowlands, Wetlands
